What each option does
A · Summary card below the calendar. Admin gets hours · days · shifts · employees. Employee gets hours · days · avg/day.
B · Summary inline under the month label. Always visible, no extra vertical space.
C · A single slot below the calendar: shows the month summary when no day is selected, flips to the day detail when one is tapped. Day-in-grid expansion is gone.
D · Thin pill-style ticker between month label and grid. Smallest footprint.
E · Same as A plus a collapsible per-employee breakdown (admin only).

Implementation notes

Option A is the safest: it's a drop-in InfoCard under the calendar in AdminScreen.kt mirroring the employee-side stats block. Option B needs a small refactor of the month-nav Row. Option C is the most work — moving DayDetailExpansion out of CalendarGrid and hoisting the selected-date slot. Option D is trivial. Option E is A plus a derived shiftsByEmployee list.
